Facilities

Large community hall, kitchen, and dedicated areas for families.

  • Community room (large, multi‑use)
  • Kitchen (events & potlucks)
  • Men’s & Women’s wudu areas
  • Shower available
  • Game room: 2 foosball, billiards, air hockey
  • On‑site parking; wheelchair friendly
